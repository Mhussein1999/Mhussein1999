## 👋 About Me

**I turn data into something decision-makers can trust and act on.**

My path into data analytics started somewhere most analysts don't: the UK Home Office, where I worked with complex case data, reviewed sensitive documentation, and made decisions where a single error had real consequences. That environment built something that can't be taught in a course — **discipline, precision, and a decision-first mindset**.

I now apply that same standard to analytics. Whether I'm cleaning a messy dataset in Excel, writing SQL queries to surface hidden patterns, or building a Power BI dashboard for a senior stakeholder — the goal is always the same: make the data reliable enough that someone can confidently act on it.

I'm particularly drawn to roles where **data, business insight, and reporting intersect** — environments where structured thinking and attention to detail genuinely move the needle.

> 🎯 Open to **Data Analyst, Reporting Analyst, and BI roles** — remote or Sheffield-based. Ready to contribute from day one.
